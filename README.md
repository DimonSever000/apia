Apia planet
A moon of Gleba with a dense and viscous atmosphere. 
Its surface consists of numerous small islands drifting in oceans of nutrient-rich royal jelly. 
Fauna is represented by a symbiosis of honey-bearing insects and invertebrate larvae.

This is my first mod for this game. If you find any issues, please let me know.
